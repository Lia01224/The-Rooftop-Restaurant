About Us

At The Rooftop Lounge we believe every meal should be an experience. We serve brunch and dinner daily. From our pasta favourites to succulent steaks and fish specials, our chefs pack every mouthful Irish flavours. Inside you’ll find a warm and welcoming space perfect for everything from casual meals to special occasions or head outside to our rooftop bar to enjoy expertly crafted drinks. Book a table now!

The Bar

Pull up a stool and get comfortable at any time, day or night to take in the incredible views at our rooftop bar. Sip on a wide selection of signature cocktails, fine wines and more while enjoying amazing views. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic favorite or an adventurous new mix, our expert bartenders have something to delight every palate. The city skyline has never tasted this good.
